    Cyberpunk 2077

    Cyberpunk 2077 takes place in a fictitious near future. It all happens in Night City. Night City is controlled by large organizations and robotics are essential in the daily life. You'll play as 'V', the main character of Cyberpunk. With the help of extensive tweaks, you can make V a man, woman, or something in between. You can customize V along the way with many augments that you can install onto V's body. Cyberpunk 2077 is a first-person shooter (FPS), which means that you control your character like your the character yourself. You can choose how to play the game and which stats to level in RPG style. These are body, intelligence, technical, reflexes, and more. There are plenty of weapons to choose from, and it's possible to play through the game without killing anybody. Cyberpunk allows you to play the game like you want.
